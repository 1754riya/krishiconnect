Frequently Asked Questions

What is कृषिCONNECT?
कृषिCONNECT delivers groceries from the farms to your doorstep in as little as a few hours! We empower you to shop at your trusted stores using our own trained and experienced Personal Shoppers, and then ensure a safe delivery to you.
Do you serve my area?
Currently we serve only in Jamshedpur. Check if we deliver to your area using our Website.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and online banking options. You can also choose to pay via COD (Cash on Delivery) for eligible orders.
Can I schedule a delivery for a later date?
Yes you can.
Is there a minimum order value?
Yes, there is a minimum order value of INR 500 for free delivery. Orders below this value will incur a delivery fee.
How do I track my order?
You can track your order in real-time using our mobile app or website. Go to the "My Orders" section and click on the order you wish to track.
